Square Covering

December 28, 2020

An odd number of $ n \times n $ squares is placed on the integer points of an infinite grid, such that any two may overlap. Show that there are at least $ n^2 $ small $ 1 \times 1 $ grid squares that are covered by an odd number of squares.

Square Covering Variations

December 28, 2020

An odd number of $ m \times n $ rectangles is placed on an infinite grid, such that any two may overlap. Show that there are at least $ mn $ small $ 1 \times 1 $ grid squares that are covered by an odd number of rectangles.
