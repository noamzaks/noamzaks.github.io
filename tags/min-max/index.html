Triangle Areas

February 11, 2021

Given a group $ S $ of points $ (x,y) \in \mathbb R ^ 2 $ such that every three points in $ S $ form a triangle (aren’t collinear) of area at most $ 1 $, show that there is some triangle with area at most 2 which bounds $ S $.
