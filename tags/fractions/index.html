Integer Grid Fractions Number Theory

We look at all of the rational numbers in $ (0, 1] $ whose denominator is smaller than some $ n \in \mathbb N $. Prove that for any two adjacent numbers $ \frac {p_i} {q_i}, \frac {p_{i+1}} {q_{i+1}} $ the following holds: $ p_{i+1} q_i - p_i q_{i+1} = 1 $.

Measure Fractions

Given a set of pairwise-disjoint intervals (such that the intersection of every pair is $ \emptyset $) in $ [0, 1] $, assuming the total length is greater than $ \frac 1 2 $, show there are two points $ a,b $ in the union of the segments such that $ |a-b| = \frac 1 {10} $.
